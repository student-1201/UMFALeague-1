Tournament Rules

OFFICIAL GUIDELINES
Points System

Win: 3 Points | Draw: 1 Point | Loss: 0 Points

Qualification Path

The top team after the group stage qualifies directly for the Grand Final. The 2nd and 3rd placed teams move to the Semifinal.

Standard Regulations

All matches follow official FIFA standard rules for fair play and conduct.

Match Timing

Morning sessions start at 06:30 AM. Evening sessions kick off at 04:00 PM precisely.
